About Me

I'm a QA Automation Consultant based in Bangalore, India, specializing in building scalable automation frameworks for Web, Mobile, and connected TV platforms including Android, FireTV, and RokuTV.

Over my career I've worked with industry leaders like GlobalLogic, Samsung R&D, and contributed to projects for global brands like Diligent, Discovery Digital Networks, and Samsung Internet Browser.

I thrive at the intersection of engineering and quality — deriving automation strategy, leading teams, and creating tooling that pushes the boundaries of what's testable. One of my proudest achievements is creating a custom AI-powered WebDriver for RokuTV — a world first.

Outside work, I'm fluent in English, Hindi, and Odia, and I enjoy mentoring engineers on automation best practices.
